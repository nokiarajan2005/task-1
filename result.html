About Us

The Department of Computer Science at Tech University is a center of excellence in computing education and research. Established with the vision to empower students in the digital age, our department offers cutting-edge knowledge in the fields of software development, data science, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and more.


We aim to create technology leaders, innovators, and responsible professionals by blending theory with hands-on practical experience. Our undergraduate and postgraduate programs are designed to equip students with the skills necessary to solve real-world problems using computational thinking and advanced technologies.


Our faculty members are highly experienced, with backgrounds in research, academia, and industry. They are committed to fostering a learner-centric environment that encourages critical thinking, collaboration, and creativity. The department also actively participates in national and international research projects and publications.


We offer students access to well-equipped laboratories, high-performance computing resources, and a strong mentor system. Students also benefit from internships, hackathons, technical fests, seminars, and workshops, all of which are integrated into our curriculum to enhance practical exposure.
